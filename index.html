Article 3: What are 3D and 4D objects.

The term 3D is a way that scientists use to describe the dimensions of an object in our 3D world. You might wondering, what do you mean by dimensions? No I don't mean dimensions of different worlds and the Multiverse, I mean the length, width and height (x,y,z) of a 3D object. Here is a 3d cube down below!

A 4D object is similar to a 3D object but is more complex. A 3D object is defined with the x,y and z coordinates however a 4D object is defined with the x,y,z and w coordinates. As a matter of fact we can not tell a 4D object because our eyes can not process the complexity of it. Here is a 4D Cube also know as a tessaract.

Article 4: What is the Solar System.

The Solar System is our home in the Milky way Galaxy. It consists of the Sun, Mecury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune and the five dwarf planets (Ceres, Pluto, Haumea, Makemake and Eris.) The Sun is our star and it is about 864,400 miles. Its Corona (Atmosphere) streches beyond the Earth. It has a magnetic field the protect the Solar system from intersteller shockwaves know as CMBs. It spin on its axis in a 22 day cycle. Because the sun is so big and not solid the equator rotates every 36 days.

Mecury, Venus, Earth and marsare the 4 rocky planets in our solar system. Mercury is the fastest planet in the solar system and completes 1 orbit every 88 Earth days but completes on rotation every 59 Earth days. Venus Is the hotest planet in the solar system. With a staggering 900°Fas surface temperature. Earth is know for it diverselife and is our home. Mars is the red rocky planet and is know for its dust storms. About 5 rovers have visited this planet and have sent intel in what it is like there.

Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune are the 4 gas giants in our solar system. Jupiter is the biggest planet in our solar system. It is a staggering 89,000 miles big. It is also know as a failed star. What I mean by that is that it is made of the same elements as the sun but is not big enough to be a star. Saturn the planet with the best rings. It also has 147 moons in orbit right now. Uranus is the 7th planet in the solar system. It is made of Hydrogen, Helium, Methane and some other elements. Neptune is know for it wild storms.

Ceres Is in the asteroid belt. Pluto, Haumea, Makemake and Eris is in the Kuiper belt.
